Volunteer for VHS Robotics Qualifier!

Whether you're experienced or not, we have a job for you.

Posted by Njeri G. on December 22, 2014

Venice Robotics will host a FIRST Tech Challenge Qualifying Tournament on Saturday, January 24, 2015 here at Venice High School. This is a great opportunity to showcase Venice High and we still need a lot of help! FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is more than just a school robotics program, itâ€™s an amazing year-long challenge focused on engaging high school students in Science, Technology, Engineering and Math by designing and programming their own robot to compete against other robots in a new engineering challenge each year.

No experience is required to volunteer; there are lots of ways to be involved on Saturday, January 24, 2015 from set-up (the night before) to snack bar to organization. We also really need those with technical, engineering or administrative experience to serve as judges, inspectors and referees. These volunteers can be FIRST FTC alums, parents, friends, relatives, retired engineers, etc. FIRST Robotics provides over-seers for the tournament and some online training is provided. It's a great way to inspire the next generation of engineers and inventors. Although our Venice team is not competing in this tournament, because we are hosting, there will be over twenty other schools and clubs competing.
