VHS Robotics Club

Official home of FTC Team 3493.

A gang of nerds.

Venice High School is where Team 3493 calls "home". Founded in 2009, the VHS Robotics club is currently competing in its sixth year with FIRST FTC. Although we spend most of our time building robots, we are passionate about engaging the Venice community in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
